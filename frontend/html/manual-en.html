Trash Type

You can regist 3 schedules each of a tarsh.

You can regist up to 10 type of trashes.

Trash Type

Enable type of trash.

  • Burnable
  • Unburnable
  • bottle
  • cans
  • plastic bottle
  • plastic
  • paper
  • recyclble
  • oversized
  • others(you can input anything name you want to regist)

Schedule Type

Schedule Type

  • every day of the week(ex. every Wedenseday)
  • day of the week in specificate week(ex. 3rd Wednseday)
  • specificate day in every week(ex. day of 10 in every month）
  • day of the week in every other week a day of the week(ex. If you can throw away trash on Sunday in 1st week, you will be able to throw away again on Sunday in 3rd week.)

Modify Schedule

If you want to modify registered schedule,please disable account link and try account link again on Alexa Skill Store or Google Assistant.

Sign in with Amazon or Google Account

You can modify schedule easily by signin with your Amazon or Google Account.


When you sign in, please click 「Signin」 button on the right up.

Next select sign in service Amazon or Google, and try signing in.

If you have already registed schedule,the schedule will be showed on the page.

Sign Out

When you sign out,please pull menu on the left up and cliek 「Sign Out」 button.
